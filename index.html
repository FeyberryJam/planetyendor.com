What is Planet Yendor?

Well, originally it was a Metroidvania game with a SNES era look and feel. I still hope to make that game someday! I bought this website to blog about my thoughts and development process but not much came of it. I then thought I'd just turn planetyendor.com into a review site that I used as a platform to babble about how much I loved the Metroidvania genre and procedural level creation and things like that. Currently however the website is primarily used for writing down a few of my thoughts on Yendor: Prelude that don't fit within Twitter's stringent character limit!

Okay then, so what is Yendor: Prelude?

Yendor: Prelude is my current project! It's what I started working on after I messed with Minecraft modding for a year and randomly got excited about working on a Metroidvania title again. I'll try to create a page dedicated to it "soon" (read: not soon) but in the mean time you can try tossing a question at my twitter account and I might get back to you there!

This website is... off putting

No... noooo it's not that bad, is it? I haven't made a website in years and while learning about html5 I bumped into jquery mobile which seems to do a lot of cool things quickly. I plan on testing the site on several different browsers on my pc before 100% committing to it. So far it looks okay to me, hopefully it's looks sort of nice on your system too.
