What is Planet Yendor?

Well, originally it was a Metroidvania game with a SNES feeling look and feel. I still hope to make that game someday! I bought this website to blog about my thoughts and development process but not much came of it. I then thought I'd just turn planetyendor.com into a review site that I used as a platform to babble about how much I loved the Metroidvania genre and procedural level creation and things like that. Currently however the website is primarily used for writing down a few of my thoughts on Yendor: Prelude that don't fit in Twitter's stringent character limit!
